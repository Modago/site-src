About Me

My background

I am an “out-going introvert” from Kenya who’s been living in Finland for 11 years. I am relatively calm person with a dream to make the world a better place, but sometimes lacking the fortitude to continuously pursue the dream ðŸ™‚. I enjoy being of service to others and never pass up an opportunity to help if I can. All in all, I am a confused … year old trying to find his place in the world a smile at a time.

After years of contemplation (and starting and stopping one or two personal blogs), it was about time to have a place to share thoughts, learn, test ideas and along the way maybe educate one or two!

I earned my PhD in information processing science from the University of Oulu in the Spring of 2018. Currently, I work part-time as a post-doctoral researcher at the OASIS research unit in the Faculty of Information Technology and Electrical Engineering, University of Oulu. My research is about the design of persuasive systems, understanding human behaviour and how technology influences and can be used to change/shape human behaviour for the better.

My research interests include:

  • Persuasive systems (design) and behaviour change.
  • Use and adoption of digital services.
  • Understanding users’ decision-making processes and psychological aspects of change.
  • Identifying ways in which technology could address the needs of the marginalised and designing digital interventions to cater to these needs.

Other interests:

  • Social Network Analysis.
  • Location-based social media analysis
  • Principles and applications of digital fabrication
    • All the files for the assignments can be found in my Gitlab account.
