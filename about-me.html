About Me

My background

I am an “out-going introvert” from Kenya who’s been living in Finland for over 10 years. I am relatively calm person with a dream to make the world a better place, but sometimes lacking the fortitude to continuously pursue the dream ðŸ™‚. I enjoy being of service to others and never pass up an opportunity to help if I can.

After years of contemplation (and starting and stopping one or two personal blogs), it was about time to have a place to share thoughts, learn, test ideas and along the way maybe educate one or two!

I earned my PHD in information processing science from the Faculty of Information Technology and Electrical Engineering, University of Oulu in the Spring of 2018. Until recently, I was working part-time as a post-doctoral researcher at the University of Oulu. Soon, I will start working on a project in collaboration with an Oulu-based urban planning and design agency. An overview of the project here.

My research has been about the design of persuasive systems, understanding human behaviour and how technology influences and can be used to change/shape human behaviour for the better.

My research interests include:

  • Persuasive systems (design).
  • Use and adoption of digital services.
  • Understanding users’ decision-making processes and psychological aspects of behaviour change.
  • Digital interventions for the marginalised in society.
  • Community-wide interventions for active mobility.

Other interests:

  • Social Network Analysis.
  • Location-based social media analysis
  • Principles and applications of digital fabrication
